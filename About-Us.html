About Dr. Siddharth Mehta

MDS | Founder & Lead Consultant, Dr. Siddharth's Dental Care

With over 8 years of clinical experience, Dr. Siddharth Mehta brings a rare combination of microscopic precision and genuine clinical warmth to every treatment. As the visionary behind Dr. Siddharth's Dental Care — located at the state-of-the-art Sun Gravitas clinic at Shyamal Cross Road, Ahmedabad — he has established a premier practice where cutting-edge technology meets elite patient comfort.

Holding both BDS and MDS degrees, Dr. Siddharth is a highly specialised dental surgeon recognised for pioneering digital workflows and advanced clinical solutions. He leverages CBCT-driven 3D imaging, digital laser dentistry, and in-house 3D printing to elevate accuracy across the entire spectrum of oral healthcare.

Clinical Expertise

  • Advanced Implantology & Restorative Surgery — Computer-guided dental implant placements and permanent crown restorations
  • Micro-Endodontics — High-precision, conservative Root Canal Treatments (RCT) to save natural tooth structures
  • Laser Dentistry — Minimally invasive laser treatments for gums, cavities and whitening
  • Smile Design & Cosmetic Dentistry — Veneers, bonding, whitening and full smile makeovers
  • Pathology & Early Detection — Oral cancer screenings and TMJ disorder management
  • Orthodontics — Braces and clear aligners for all ages
