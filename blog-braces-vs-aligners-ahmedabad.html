Braces vs Clear Aligners — The Complete 2026 Comparison

Modern orthodontic options — braces and aligners in Ahmedabad

One of the most common questions at our Ahmedabad clinic: 'Should I get braces or clear aligners?' Both straighten teeth effectively but are very different in cost, comfort and lifestyle.

Traditional Metal Braces

💰 Cost in Ahmedabad: ₹25,000–₹45,000
👁 Visibility: Visible metal brackets and wires
⏱ Duration: 18 months–3 years
✅ Best for: Complex cases, children, budget-conscious patients
Professional orthodontic consultation in Ahmedabad

Clear Aligners (Invisible Braces)

💰 Cost in Ahmedabad: ₹60,000–₹1,50,000
👁 Visibility: Nearly invisible
⏱ Duration: 6–18 months for most cases
✅ Best for: Adults, professionals, mild to moderate crowding

Which should you choose?

If your case is complex or you have children, braces are more reliable and affordable. If you are an adult who wants a discreet solution, clear aligners are excellent. The best way to decide is a consultation at our Ahmedabad clinic.
